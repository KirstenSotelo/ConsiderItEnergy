Our Services

Comprehensive energy solutions tailored to your home's needs

What We Offer

Expert energy services to make your home more efficient and comfortable

Existing Homes

We offer EnerGuide home energy evaluations to help you understand your home's energy usage. Our assessments identify retrofits that can make your home warmer, reduce energy bills, and lower your carbon footprint.

Qualify for federal, provincial, and municipal grants, rebates, and interest-free loans.

Learn more

HVAC Design

Our team performs heat loss and heat gain calculations to size mechanical equipment accurately. Properly sized equipment ensures optimal performance and energy efficiency.

We design your home's air delivery system for maximum comfort and efficiency.

Learn more

New Construction

We provide energy modeling and efficiency planning for new homes, ensuring designs meet or exceed energy efficiency standards. Our services include compliance with the National Building Code's energy requirements.

Optimize insulation, ventilation, and heating systems to minimize energy consumption.

Learn more

Blower Door Testing

We offer state-of-the-art blower door testing to determine your home's air tightness, a cost-effective method to enhance energy efficiency and achieve higher energy tiers.

Our automated testing includes wind sensing technology and geotagging for precise results.

Learn more
