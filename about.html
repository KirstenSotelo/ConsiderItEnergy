About Consider it Energy

Helping homeowners achieve energy efficiency and sustainability through expert evaluation and innovative solutions.

Our Story

At Consider it Energy, our goal is to be the leading "One Stop Shop" for energy evaluations, helping homeowners and builders navigate the complexities of grants, permits, and energy compliance.

We believe in collaborative growth and success, supporting customers by providing services that help homeowners qualify for government grants, ensure new construction meets energy standards, and improve overall energy efficiency.

As sustainability becomes a priority, we make it easier for clients to meet regulatory standards, reduce delays, and achieve their energy goals.

Shawn Sales

Founder & Energy Advisor

Shawn Sales has over 15 years of experience in sales management and a strong commitment to sustainability. After transitioning to the sustainable energy sector in 2022, he earned a diploma in Sustainable Energy Technology and became a Registered Energy Advisor.

Shawn specializes in solar PV, geo-exchange, wind energy, and HVAC systems, and is skilled in energy evaluations, Net Zero solutions, heat load calculations, and duct design.

As a Quality Assurance Specialist and Service Organization Manager, Shawn trains and supports energy advisors, ensuring compliance with energy performance standards. His diverse background in retail and construction, combined with his technical expertise, allows him to provide effective, innovative energy solutions.

Shawn is passionate about reducing environmental impact through sustainable energy and continues to expand his knowledge through various certifications in energy efficiency and sustainable technology. At Consider It Energy, his goal is to help homeowners and builders achieve their sustainability goals.
