Our Services

Comprehensive energy solutions for your home

Existing Homes

We offer EnerGuide home energy evaluations and issue reports that help you understand how your home uses energy. Identify retrofits that create warmer homes, reduce energy bills, and lower your carbon footprint.

Qualify for federal, provincial, and municipal grants, rebates and interest-free loans.

Learn More

HVAC Design

We perform heat loss and heat gain calculations to size mechanical equipment. Properly sized equipment should be paired with properly designed ventilation.

Our team is trained to design your home air delivery system for optimum comfort.

Learn More

New Construction

We provide energy modeling and efficiency planning for newly built homes. Our expertise ensures that your new home is designed with sustainability in mind, optimizing insulation, ventilation, and heating systems to minimize energy consumption.

Reduce your dependence on fossil fuels while saving money long-term.
